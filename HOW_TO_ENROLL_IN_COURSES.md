# üìö How to Enroll in Discipleship Training Courses

## Quick Guide for Users

This guide explains how to enroll in discipleship training courses in the Greater Works City Church app.

---

## üéØ Step-by-Step Enrollment Process

### Step 1: Navigate to Discipleship & Training

1. Open the app and make sure you're logged in
2. Go to the **More** tab (bottom navigation)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Discipleship & Training"** (purple icon with school symbol)

### Step 2: Browse Available Courses

1. You'll see the **"Courses"** tab selected by default
2. Browse through available courses
3. Each course card shows:
   - Course title and instructor
   - Course description
   - Duration and number of lessons
   - Number of enrolled students
   - Category and difficulty level

### Step 3: View Course Details (Optional)

1. **Tap on any course card** to view full details
2. The course detail modal shows:
   - Full description
   - Instructor information
   - Course duration
   - Total lessons
   - Enrollment count

### Step 4: Enroll in a Course

You can enroll in two ways:

#### Option A: From Course Card
1. On the course card, tap the **"Enroll Now"** button (blue button)
2. A confirmation dialog will appear
3. Tap **"Enroll"** to confirm
4. You'll see a success message: "You have been enrolled in the course!"

#### Option B: From Course Details Modal
1. Tap on a course card to open details
2. Scroll through the course information
3. Tap the **"Enroll Now"** button at the bottom
4. Confirm enrollment in the dialog
5. You'll see a success message

---

## ‚úÖ After Enrollment

### What Happens Next?

1. **Enrollment Confirmed**: You'll receive a success message
2. **Button Changes**: The "Enroll Now" button changes to "Enrolled" (green)
3. **Access to Lessons**: You can now view and access course lessons
4. **Progress Tracking**: Your progress is automatically tracked

### Viewing Your Enrolled Courses

1. Go to **Discipleship & Training** screen
2. Tap the **"My Progress"** tab
3. You'll see all your enrolled courses with:
   - Progress percentage
   - Number of completed lessons
   - Completion status
   - Certificate badge (when completed)

### Accessing Course Lessons

1. Go to **Discipleship & Training** ‚Üí **"Courses"** tab
2. Tap on an enrolled course (button shows "Enrolled")
3. In the course details modal, you'll see:
   - **Course Lessons** section
   - List of all lessons
   - Checkmarks for completed lessons
4. Tap any lesson to view content

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Can I enroll in multiple courses?
**Yes!** You can enroll in as many courses as you want.

### What if I'm already enrolled?
- The button will show **"Enrolled"** (green)
- Tapping it shows: "You are already enrolled in this course"
- You can still access the course and view lessons

### Can I unenroll from a course?
Currently, enrollment is permanent. Contact admin if you need to unenroll.

### Do I need internet to enroll?
**Yes**, enrollment requires internet connection to save to the database.

### What if enrollment fails?
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try again - the error message will guide you
